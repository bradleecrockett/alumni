Mission

Welcome to the Roseville High School Alumni's official page (California). This site is dedicated to all current and former students and staff of Roseville High School.
If you are interested in showing your Tiger Pride and supporting Roseville High, consider joining the alumni association. Our mission is to support the school and each other as a community.

History

The RHSAA was established in February of 1991 to provide support for students, faculty and community by funding Scholarships for graduating seniors and the Teacher's Wish List. We also donate to Senior Grad Night, the Rose Committee, PTC and much more. As "keepers of the old Tiger traditions" we're working to bring back our beloved Alma Mater, Fight Song and getting the community involved in supporting future generations of Tiger Alumni. Your yearly membership dollars and donations along with several fundraisers held throughout the year are what help to fund these programs. Membership does not require participation for those that want to join and stay behind the scenes. However, for those that do like to get involved we're always on the lookout for event volunteers, Committee Chairs, Board Members, etc... Your level of involvement is completely up to you!


So join the RHSAA today for $5.00 a year and remember...
